Story of Elder Tree Woodworking

At Elder Tree Woodworking we bring old, discarded, reclaimed materials along with some new materials and turn them into unique, one-of-a-kind, functional pieces. Each piece is handcrafted and is as individual as the material we use. Some wood may have knots, imperfections in the grain, or be "discolored." Those imperfections just add to the character of the piece to make it more interesting and fun! Contact me for a quote and design consultation for furniture pieces as unique as you and your home!

About Mark

I am a 3rd generation carpenter and after working for other people and being a boss for others, it is now time to be my own boss. I love to design and build the kind of pieces that not only help the environment by reclaiming old or used material, but are one of a kind and really cool too! All pieces are crafted by hand and Made in the USA in northwestern Illinois in my personal workshop.

Offerings

We offer indoor furniture pieces, such as custom coffee tables, sofa tables, end tables, solid piece dining tables, and much, much more. Decorative pieces are also available for your indoor living including framed mirrors, wall hangings, shelves, and cabinets. Outdoor living is also important to us, so our shop includes such pieces as custom concrete table tops, planter window boxes, birdhouses and more.
